# Skill 01 — Find Trending ADHD Topics

## Purpose
Research current ADHD parenting topics and produce a prioritized content opportunity brief.

## Target User
The ADHD Parenting project owner.

## Inputs
- Current ADHD parenting discussions
- YouTube topic searches
- Search trends
- Trusted ADHD resources
- Parent questions
- Existing content performance, when available

## Output
A daily topic opportunity report with:
- Top 10 ADHD parenting content opportunities
- Scores
- Suggested content formats
- Suggested hooks
- Monetization notes

## Research Categories
- Homework battles
- Morning routines
- Bedtime and sleep
- Emotional regulation
- Executive function
- School accommodations
- Medication questions
- Nutrition and exercise
- Parent burnout
- Organization
- Screen time
- Teen ADHD
- Self-esteem

## Scoring Criteria
Each topic is scored 1–5:
1. Parent pain intensity
2. Trend potential
3. Evergreen value
4. Content format flexibility
5. Monetization potential
6. Safety and trust fit

Total score: 30 points.

## Output Format
| Rank | Topic | Category | Why it matters | Source signals | Score | Best format | Suggested hook |
|---|---|---|---|---|---:|---|---|

## Runtime Prompt
Find current ADHD parenting topic opportunities. Prioritize topics that are practical, emotionally relevant, evergreen, and suitable for Instagram Reels, carousels, YouTube Shorts, newsletters, and future digital products. Avoid medical claims and include source links when available.

## Constraints
- Do not diagnose.
- Do not provide medical advice.
- Do not claim something is trending unless current sources were checked.
- Do not use shame-based language.
- Do not scrape private communities.
