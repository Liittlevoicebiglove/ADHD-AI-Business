# Skill 02 — Parent Question Finder

## Purpose
Collect real questions parents are asking about ADHD and group them into content opportunities.

## Inputs
- Public forums
- YouTube comments when available
- Q&A sites
- Social comments when available
- Email replies in the future

## Output
A grouped list of parent questions.

## Output Format
| Category | Parent Question | Pain Point | Content Angle | Suggested Format |
|---|---|---|---|---|

## Runtime Prompt
Find recurring questions from parents of children with ADHD. Group similar questions together and turn each group into a content angle. Keep the tone compassionate and practical.

## Constraints
- Do not include private personal information.
- Do not shame parent wording.
- Do not present individual stories as universal facts.
