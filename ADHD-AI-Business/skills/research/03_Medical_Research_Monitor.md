# Skill 03 — Medical Research Monitor

## Purpose
Monitor trusted ADHD sources and summarize new or useful research in plain language.

## Trusted Source Types
- CDC
- NIH
- CHADD
- AAP
- Understood
- ADDitude
- Peer-reviewed journals when available

## Output
Plain-language research notes that can inspire safe educational content.

## Output Format
| Source | Topic | Plain-language summary | Parent takeaway | Content idea | Safety note |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

## Runtime Prompt
Review trusted ADHD and child development sources for new or useful information. Summarize findings in parent-friendly language. Include citations or links when available. Clearly separate education from medical advice.

## Constraints
- Do not provide diagnosis or treatment advice.
- Do not overstate research findings.
- Do not use sensational claims.
