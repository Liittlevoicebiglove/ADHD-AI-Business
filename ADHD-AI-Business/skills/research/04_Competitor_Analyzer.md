# Skill 04 — Competitor Analyzer

## Purpose
Analyze public ADHD parenting creators and identify content patterns, gaps, hooks, and opportunities.

## Inputs
- Public Instagram posts
- Public YouTube videos
- Public websites/blogs
- Public newsletters when available

## Output
Competitive insights and content gaps.

## Output Format
| Creator/Source | Topic | Format | Hook Pattern | Engagement Signal | Gap | Opportunity |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

## Runtime Prompt
Analyze public ADHD parenting content. Identify what topics and formats appear to be performing well, what hooks are commonly used, and where there are gaps this brand can fill with practical, parent-friendly content.

## Constraints
- Do not copy creator content.
- Do not plagiarize hooks.
- Use competitor research only for strategic insight.
