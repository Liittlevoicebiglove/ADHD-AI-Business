# Vision

## Brand Vision
Create a trusted ADHD parenting resource that helps overwhelmed parents feel calmer, more informed, and more capable.

## Audience
Parents and caregivers of children with ADHD who want practical daily strategies without shame, judgment, or medical overwhelm.

## Positioning
Practical ADHD parenting support for real families.

## Promise
We help parents turn daily ADHD struggles into simple routines, better communication, and more confidence.

## What This Brand Is
- Helpful
- Compassionate
- Practical
- Parent-focused
- Evidence-informed
- Non-judgmental

## What This Brand Is Not
- Medical diagnosis
- Medication advice
- Fear-based parenting content
- Shame-based discipline content
- Anti-teacher, anti-doctor, or anti-parent messaging
