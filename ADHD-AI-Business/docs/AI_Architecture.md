# AI Architecture

## Roles

### ChatGPT
Strategist, editor, planning assistant, and high-level business architect.

### Claude
Long-form writing, content expansion, documentation improvement, and repository maintenance.

### Zapier MCP
Execution and orchestration layer that connects apps and runs repeatable skills.

### GitHub
Source of truth for documentation, prompts, skills, workflows, and templates.

## Workflow

Research signals feed content planning.
Content planning feeds creation.
Creation feeds publishing.
Publishing feeds analytics.
Analytics feeds future research.

## Human Approval
Publishing, medical-sensitive content, monetization offers, and brand changes should require manual approval.
