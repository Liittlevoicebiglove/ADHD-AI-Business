# ADHD AI Business

A working repository for building a semi-automated ADHD Parenting content and monetization system.

## Mission
Build a trusted ADHD parenting brand that helps parents understand, support, and advocate for children with ADHD through practical, compassionate, evidence-informed content.

## Primary Business Goal
Grow into a sustainable content business that can eventually generate $2,000–$4,000/month through digital products, affiliate offers, email marketing, and long-form content.

## Core System
1. Research engine
2. Content engine
3. Creative asset engine
4. Publishing workflow
5. Analytics loop
6. Monetization engine
